About Me

I'm Ali and I am a Journalism + Design student at The New School Eugene Lang College of Liberal Arts. I was born on Long Island here in New York, but I grew up in Hawaii on the island of Maui. I took some time after high school to reevaluate what exactly I wanted out of life. I had two options: live on the island I've always known, go to local community college, and probably settle down early; or, I could uproot my comfortable lifestyle at home and move clear across the globe to New York City. Obviously my choice is clear.

Leaving home was the best decision I've ever made. I'm attending the school of my dreams, meeting people from all walks of life, and living in a city with endless opportunity. Stay tuned to my blog for updates and observations about my new life in the big city!
